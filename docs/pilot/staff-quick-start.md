# Verve Operational Suite — Clinical Staff Quick Start Guide

**Version:** 1.0  
**Audience:** Clinical Staff  
**Classification:** Internal — Pilot  
**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Welcome to Verve

Verve is the internal platform your clinic is trialling to manage rosters, timesheets, and leave. This guide explains everything you need to know to get started quickly.

This is a pilot. We expect questions — please use the support process at the end of this guide to ask them.

---

## 1. Logging In

### What You Need Before You Start

- Your **work email address** (provided during onboarding)
- Your **temporary password** (provided by your Practice Manager or Pilot Administrator)
- A **smartphone** for MFA setup (if required — see Section 2)
- A web browser: **Chrome** or **Edge** work best

### First-Time Login Steps

1. Open your browser and go to the Verve URL shared with you
2. Enter your **email address** and **temporary password**
3. You will be asked to **set a new password**:
   - At least 12 characters
   - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
4. After setting your password, proceed to MFA setup if prompted (see Section 2)

### Logging In After Setup

1. Go to the Verve URL
2. Enter your email and password
3. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
4. You're in

### Forgotten Password

1. On the login page, select **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (expires in 15 minutes)
4. Follow the link and set a new password

> If you are locked out of your account, contact your Practice Manager straight away.

## 2. MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication)

MFA adds a second layer of security to your account. Depending on your role, MFA may or may not be required during the pilot.

Your Practice Manager will advise you whether this step applies to you.

### If MFA Is Required for You

1. After your first login, you will see a **Set Up MFA** screen
2. Download one of the following apps on your phone (free):
   - **Google Authenticator** (iOS / Android)
   - **Microsoft Authenticator** (iOS / Android)
3. In the Verve MFA screen, select **Set Up Authenticator App**
4. Open your app and scan the **QR code** shown on your screen
5. Your app will now show a 6-digit code — enter it in Verve to confirm
6. **Save the backup codes shown** — write them down or store them somewhere safe. If you lose your phone, you'll need these.
7. Select **Complete Setup**

From now on, every login will ask for a 6-digit code from your app.

### Lost Your Phone?

Tell your Practice Manager immediately. They will contact the Pilot Administrator to reset your MFA. Do not try to work around it.

## 3. Viewing Your Roster

1. Log in to Verve
2. From the menu, select **Roster** (or **My Roster**)
3. You will see your scheduled shifts for the current week
4. Use the **week navigation arrows** to view upcoming or past weeks

### What You Can and Cannot Do

| Can Do | Cannot Do |
|--------|-----------|
| View your own rostered shifts | Edit your own roster |
| View the current and upcoming weeks | View other clinics' rosters |
| See your shift times and role | Approve or publish rosters |

> If your roster appears incorrect, speak to your Practice Manager — do not attempt to change it yourself.

## 4. Submitting a Timesheet

Timesheets record the hours you actually worked. Submit them at the end of each day or shift, or as instructed by your Practice Manager.

### How to Submit

1. From the menu, select **Timesheets**
2. Select **New Timesheet Entry** (or the equivalent button on your timesheet view)
3. Select the **date** for the entry
4. Enter:
   - **Start time** — when you began work
   - **End time** — when you finished
   - **Break duration** (if applicable)
   - **Notes** — optional; use for anything your manager should know
5. Select **Submit**

### Timesheet Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Submitted | Sent to your Practice Manager for review |
| Approved | Confirmed by your Practice Manager |
| Returned | Your manager has asked you to correct something |

### If a Timesheet Is Returned

1. Go to **Timesheets** → **My Timesheets**
2. Select the returned entry
3. Read your manager's note
4. Make the correction
5. Resubmit

> Do not submit duplicate timesheets. If you are unsure whether a submission went through, check **My Timesheets** for its status.

## 5. Submitting a Leave Request

1. From the menu, select **Leave**
2. Select **New Leave Request**
3. Choose the **leave type**:
   - Annual Leave
   - Sick Leave
   - Other (specify in notes)
4. Select your **start date** and **end date**
5. Add any **notes** for your manager (optional, but helpful for sick leave)
6. Select **Submit**

### Leave Request Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Submitted | Waiting for Practice Manager review |
| Approved | Leave confirmed |
| Declined | Leave not approved (reason provided by your manager) |

> Plan ahead for annual leave — submit requests at least 2 weeks in advance where possible. Sick leave should be submitted as soon as practicable.

## 6. Inventory Visibility

Depending on your role, you may have **read-only** access to the clinic's inventory.

If this applies to you:

1. From the menu, select **Inventory**
2. You can view current stock levels and product details
3. You **cannot** make adjustments — this is for Practice Managers only

If you believe a product is running low and no adjustment has been made, tell your Practice Manager directly.

## 7. Expected Behaviour During the Pilot

During the pilot, you may encounter:

| Situation | What It Means | What to Do |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| A screen looks different than expected | The interface may have been updated | Proceed normally; report if confusing |
| A feature is missing or not working | A known gap or bug | Report it — don't try to force it |
| Your data doesn't look right | Possible entry error or system issue | Do not change it; report it |
| The system is slow or unresponsive | May be a system load issue | Wait 30 seconds and refresh; report if persists |
| You can't log in | Could be password, MFA, or account issue | Contact Practice Manager immediately |

### What This Platform Is Not

- It is **not** a patient management system — no patient records go into Verve
- It is **not** connected to payroll yet — timesheets are recorded but not yet processed
- It is **not** available on a mobile app yet — use a desktop or laptop browser

## 8. Support Process

If something goes wrong or isn't working:

### Step 1 — Note What Happened

Before reporting, write down:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- The time it happened
- Any error message you saw (a screenshot is ideal)

### Step 2 — Contact Your Practice Manager

Most issues can be resolved or escalated by your Practice Manager. Tell them:
- What you experienced
- Whether it stopped you from doing your work

### Step 3 — If Urgent

If the issue prevents you from completing your shift-critical tasks and your Practice Manager is unavailable, contact the Pilot Administrator (Daniel Blanco) using the contact details provided to your clinic.

### What to Expect

| Severity | Response Target |
|----------|----------------|
| Can't log in / system down | < 2 hours |
| Key function not working | < 8 hours (business hours) |
| Minor issue / question | Next business day |

> The pilot team wants to hear about problems. Reporting issues is not a complaint — it helps make the platform better for everyone.
