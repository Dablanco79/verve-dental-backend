## Welcome

This guide walks you through the key tasks you'll perform in Verve during the pilot. Each section covers one area of the platform with step-by-step instructions.

If something does not match what you see on screen, or you encounter an error, please report it using the process in `support-process.md`.

---

## 1. Login

### First-Time Login

1. Open your web browser (Chrome or Edge recommended)
2. Navigate to the Verve URL provided by your Pilot Administrator
3. Enter your **email address** and the **temporary password** provided during onboarding
4. You will be prompted to **change your password** on first login
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - At least one uppercase, one lowercase, one number, one special character
5. After changing your password, you will be directed to MFA setup (see Section 2)

### Subsequent Logins

1. Enter your email and password
2. Open your authenticator app
3. Enter the 6-digit code shown
4. You are now logged in

### Forgotten Password

1. On the login page, select **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 15 minutes)
4. Follow the link and set a new password

> **Note:** If you are locked out (too many failed attempts), contact your Pilot Administrator to have your account unlocked.

## 2. MFA Setup

MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) is **mandatory** for all Practice Managers. This step occurs once, immediately after your first login.

### Setup Steps

1. After first login, you will see a **Set Up MFA** screen
2. Download an authenticator app on your smartphone if you do not already have one:
   - **Google Authenticator** (iOS / Android)
   - **Microsoft Authenticator** (iOS / Android)
   - **Authy** (iOS / Android)
3. In the Verve MFA setup screen, select **Set Up Authenticator App**
4. Open your authenticator app and scan the **QR code** shown on screen
5. Enter the **6-digit code** generated by your app to confirm setup
6. Save the **backup codes** shown — store them securely (e.g. a locked drawer or password manager). These allow access if you lose your phone.
7. Select **Complete Setup**

### If You Lose Your Authenticator Device

Contact your Pilot Administrator immediately. Do not attempt to work around MFA.

## 3. Inventory Management

### Viewing Stock Levels

1. From the main menu, select **Inventory**
2. You will see a list of all products assigned to your clinic
3. Use the **search bar** to find a specific product
4. Use the **filter** options to view by category, low stock, or expiry status

### Recording a Stock Adjustment

Use this when you physically count stock and need to correct the system quantity.

1. Navigate to **Inventory**
2. Find the product to adjust
3. Select **Adjust Stock**
4. Choose the **adjustment type**:
   - **Addition** — stock received or found
   - **Reduction** — stock used, damaged, expired, or missing
5. Enter the **quantity** being adjusted
6. Enter a **reason** (required)
7. Select **Confirm Adjustment**

> The adjustment is recorded immediately with your name, date, and reason. Adjustments cannot be deleted — if you make an error, submit a correcting adjustment.

### Viewing Inventory History

1. Navigate to **Inventory**
2. Select a product
3. Select the **History** tab
4. View all adjustments, dates, users, and reasons

## 4. Materials Forecast

The forecast module uses your historical usage data to recommend reorder quantities.

### Viewing Forecasts

1. From the main menu, select **Materials Forecast** (or **Forecast**)
2. You will see a forecast summary for your clinic
3. Each row shows:
   - Product name
   - Current stock
   - Forecast usage (next 30 days)
   - Recommended order quantity
   - Days until stockout (estimated)

### Reviewing Recommendations

- Items flagged **red** are at immediate reorder risk — consider creating a Purchase Order promptly
- Items flagged **amber** are approaching threshold — monitor weekly
- Items flagged **green** are within safe levels

> The forecast is a recommendation only. You retain full discretion over what and when to order.

## 5. Purchase Orders

### Creating a Purchase Order

1. Navigate to **Procurement** → **Purchase Orders**
2. Select **New Purchase Order**
3. Choose your **supplier** from the list
4. Add line items:
   - Search for product
   - Enter quantity
   - Verify unit price (editable if required)
5. Review the order summary
6. Select **Submit for Approval** (or **Save as Draft** if not ready)

### Approving a Purchase Order

If you have approval authority:

1. Navigate to **Procurement** → **Pending Approval**
2. Review the order details
3. Select **Approve** or **Reject**
4. If rejecting, enter a reason (required)

### Viewing Purchase Order History

1. Navigate to **Procurement** → **Purchase Orders**
2. Use the **status filter** to view: Draft, Pending, Approved, Received, Rejected

## 6. Roster Management

### Viewing the Roster

1. From the main menu, select **Roster**
2. Select the **week** using the date navigation
3. You will see all staff rostered at your clinic for that week

### Editing the Roster

1. Navigate to **Roster**
2. Select the week you want to edit
3. Select a **staff member's name**
4. Select **Edit Shift** on the relevant day
5. Adjust start time, end time, or role
6. Select **Save**

> Changes to published rosters are visible immediately to staff.

### Publishing a Roster

Rosters must be **published** before staff can see them:

1. Navigate to **Roster**
2. Select the target week
3. Review all shifts
4. Select **Publish Roster**
5. Confirm the action

> Once published, staff receive visibility. Notify staff separately if significant changes are made post-publication.

## 7. Timesheet Approval

### Viewing Pending Timesheets

1. From the main menu, select **Timesheets**
2. Select the **Pending Approval** tab
3. You will see a list of submitted timesheets awaiting your review

### Approving or Rejecting a Timesheet

1. Select a timesheet from the list
2. Review:
   - Date and time entries
   - Any notes from the staff member
   - Comparison to the rostered shift (shown alongside)
3. Select **Approve** or **Return for Correction**
4. If returning for correction, enter a reason (required)

> Timesheets should be reviewed within **24 hours** of submission during the pilot. Flag any discrepancies against roster records rather than modifying them yourself.

---

## 8. Leave Approval

### Viewing Pending Leave Requests

1. From the main menu, select **Leave**
2. Select the **Pending Approval** tab

### Approving or Rejecting Leave

1. Select the leave request
2. Review:
   - Leave type (annual, sick, other)
   - Dates requested
   - Any staff notes
   - Current roster coverage for those dates
3. Select **Approve** or **Decline**
4. If declining, enter a reason (required)

> Declined leave requests are visible to staff. Use professional, clear language in your reason.

## 9. Analytics

### Accessing Dashboards

1. From the main menu, select **Analytics**
2. Choose your dashboard view:
   - **Clinic Overview** — key operational metrics for your clinic
   - **Inventory Summary** — stock levels, adjustments, and low-stock trends
   - **Workforce Summary** — roster coverage, timesheet status, leave balances

### Interpreting the Data

- Dashboards refresh on a scheduled basis (typically every few hours)
- Data reflects your clinic only — you cannot view other clinics
- Use the **date range selector** to view historical periods

> During the pilot, report any figures that appear incorrect via the support process. Do not make operational decisions based on data you suspect is erroneous.

## 10. Common Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to Do |
|-------|-----------|
| Cannot log in | Check email/password; use Forgot Password if needed; contact Pilot Admin if still locked out |
| MFA code rejected | Ensure your device clock is accurate (sync time in phone settings); try a fresh code |
| Page not loading | Refresh the browser; clear cache; try a different browser |
| Data appears incorrect | Do not edit to "fix" — report via support process with screenshot |
| Unable to approve a timesheet | Check you have the correct role; contact Pilot Admin |
| Purchase order not saving | Check all required fields are complete; check your internet connection |
| Roster not visible to staff | Confirm roster is published, not in draft |
| Stock adjustment shows wrong qty | Submit a correcting adjustment; report via support for investigation |

### What NOT to Do During the Pilot

- Do not share your login credentials with anyone
- Do not attempt to access another clinic's data
- Do not delete records or attempt to undo completed actions
- Do not attempt to bypass MFA
- Do not make bulk changes without consulting your Pilot Administrator
